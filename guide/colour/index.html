Colour

Light colours

Light colours use the bt- (background tint) prefix in their classnames.

bt-white
$colour-white
#fff

bt-light-grey
$colour-light-grey
#f2f2f2

bt-pale-yellow
$colour-pale-yellow
#F6F4D0

bt-highlight-yellow
$colour-highlight-yellow
#F7FD54

bt-sage-green
$colour-sage-green
#60CC7C

Dark colours

Dark colours use the bg- (background) prefix in their classnames.

bg-black
$colour-black
#010111

bg-interactive-blue
$colour-interactive-blue
#2e38ef
